# Feed the Animal

## Overview
A cute educational mini-game for children aged 2–6 years old.

Core concept:
- Animals appear hungry.
- The child feeds the correct food to the animal.
- Correct feeding triggers happy animations, sounds, and reactions.

The game teaches:
- Animal recognition
- Food recognition
- Matching logic
- Cause & effect
- Vocabulary learning

The emotional experience should feel:
- Warm
- Funny
- Rewarding
- Interactive
- Adorable

---

# Core Gameplay

## Main Loop

### Step 1 — Animal Appears
A cute animal appears on screen.

Example:
- Rabbit
- Monkey
- Panda
- Cat
- Elephant

The animal shows:
- Hungry face
- Belly growl animation
- Sad/cute expression

Voice:
> “The rabbit is hungry!”

---

### Step 2 — Food Choices Appear
3–4 food items appear below.

Example:
Rabbit stage:
- Carrot ✅
- Fish
- Banana
- Bamboo

The child drags food toward the animal.

---

### Step 3 — Validate Choice

## Correct Food

When correct food is dropped:

### Animal Reaction
- Eats food animation
- Happy dance
- Smile expression
- Jumping animation
- Tail wagging
- Chewing animation

### Visual Effects
- Hearts
- Stars
- Rainbow sparkles
- Confetti

### Audio
- Eating sound
- Happy sound
- “Yummy!”
- “Great job!”

### Learning Feedback
Voice pronunciation:
> “Carrot!”

or:
> “Rabbit likes carrots!”

---

## Wrong Food

When incorrect food is dropped:

### Animal Reaction
- Confused face
- Funny rejection animation
- Head shake

### Visual Feedback
- Food bounces away
- Small puff animation
- Gentle shake

### Audio
- Soft “Oops!”
- Silly sound effect

Important:
- No punishment
- No scary sounds
- Encourage retry
